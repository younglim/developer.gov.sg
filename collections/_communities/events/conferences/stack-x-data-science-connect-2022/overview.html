This July, we're bringing you a 2-day virtual conference where you can learn how the Singapore Government is driving data transformation and delivering public good through the use of Data Science and AI.

Be inspired by real use cases from government and industry speakers. Plus, discover collaboration opportunities available for government agencies and industry players.

Who should attend?

We welcome data and tech professionals such as Agency CIOs and IT Directors, Project Managers, Business Analysts, Developers and Solution Architects from Government Agencies and industry to join us for this conference.
