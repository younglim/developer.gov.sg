Overview

From streamlining business processes, to saving users from remembering multiple login credentials, Singpass is a key enabler for achieving a digital economy. As part of the National Digital Identity (NDI) initiative, GovTech works with industry partners to extend the use of Singpass products.

The NDI Industry Engagement Office and Assurity Trusted Solutions will share how different businesses can leverage Singpass to develop value-added services for their customers.

Also covered are use cases of Singpass adoption in HealthCerts, a set of digital standards and schema enabling the issuance and verification of digital documents that are tamperproof and Accredify START, a healthcare portal facilitating safe return to work.
