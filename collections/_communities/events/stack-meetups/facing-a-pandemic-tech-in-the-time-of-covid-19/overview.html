Overview

Covid-19, the epidemic no one saw coming. But adversity and opportunity are two sides of the same coin. Join us in this webinar, as we find out more about how companies and governmental outfits are pivoting and innovating to meet these new challenges head-on.

How is tech being tapped on, for efficacy, impact, and ultimately for the greater good?
